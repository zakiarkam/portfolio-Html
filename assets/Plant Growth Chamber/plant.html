Introduction

In the quest for healthier diets and sustainable living, urban agriculture has become more critical than ever. But challenges like limited space, unpredictable weather, and transportation hurdles often hinder our access to fresh leafy greens, leaving a gap in our nutritional needs. That's where our groundbreaking project comes in! Our team has developed an ingenious solution to transform the way we grow and enjoy leafy greens. Imagine a controlled indoor growing system that nurtures seeds and plants under optimal conditions, tailored for each stage of growth. Our mission is clear: to boost productivity, enhance nutrition, and bring fresh greens closer to every plate.

Functions

  • Temperature Precision: Our microcontroller-driven watering system ensures optimal temperature using DHT22 sensors.
  • Moisture Management: Capacitive soil moisture sensors activate watering only when necessary.
  • Humidity Harmony: Cooling fans manage humidity, ensuring a conducive growth environment.
  • Customized Light Cycles: Light system guided by RTC DS3231 for optimal photosynthesis.
  • LCD Insights: Real-time data display on humidity, temperature, and moisture levels.
